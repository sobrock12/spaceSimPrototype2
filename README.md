# spaceSimPrototype2

A VR game made in Unity. You are the pilot of an agile spaceship cruising throughout a newly discovered and bustling solar system.
Navigate the vast frontier via a fast and reactive 6 degrees of freedom flight model and complete missions to gain more credits and increase your status.
Take on space pirates and mysterious inter-dimensional aliens with various weapons and loadouts you can customize to fit your style!
Mine asteroids and clean up space junk for big profits!

Currently in prototyping/early alpha stages. Source code can be downloaded and played through Unity editor. Compiled release builds will be available soon.
